THON

THON is a student-run philanthropy committed to enhancing the lives of children and families impacted by childhood cancer. Our mission is to provide emotional and financial support, spread awareness, and ensure funding for critical research—all in pursuit of a cure.

I am going into my fourth year as a THON Volunteer. The first two years, I was on Rules & Regulations committees, providing security for various THON events throughout the year. Last year, I was a Dash Developer on the Technology Committee. Dash is our event management system, primarily used THON Weekend. It tracks building capacity, floor admittance, dancer wellness, and now we can track the state of our fire exits! The last one is a project I completed on short notice before THON Weekend.

Fire Exits

A week before THON, we were given a project by Rules & Regulations to move the Exit Safety Binder from using a physical binder to Dash. I worked with one of my Co-Captains to make a four page Vue form, a table to display the results, and another table to display all completed forms across the entire weekend.
